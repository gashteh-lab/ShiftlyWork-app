Features

Built for the real world
of shift work

Every feature designed around how shift workers actually live — from auto-scheduling to fatigue tracking.

📅

Intelligent Shift Calendar

Day, Night, Off — colour-coded across your entire month. Tap any day to see start time, end time, and duration. Override or lock any date without disrupting the rest of your pattern.

Colour-coded shifts Tap for details Override any day Lock dates PDF export
✨

Shift Planner

Choose a rotation pattern and auto-fill up to 10 years in one tap. A live 30-day colour preview shows exactly how your shifts will look before you generate. Supports 2D·2N·4OFF, 3D·3N·3OFF, 4D·4OFF, and fully custom patterns.

2D·2N·4OFF 3D·3N·3OFF 4D·4OFF Custom patterns Up to 10-year fill
⏰

Smart Shift Alarms

Pick a date range and Shiftly finds every upcoming shift and creates iOS system alarms automatically. Separate alarm times for Day shifts and Night shifts — set them once, never touch them again.

One-tap setup 21+ alarms at once Per shift type Native iOS alarms
💰

Payroll Estimator

See your estimated gross pay before payday. Shiftly automatically calculates base pay, night differential, weekend premiums, and overtime — all broken down from your actual shift schedule.

Night differential Weekend premium Overtime Manual bonuses Hourly rate config
🧠

Fatigue Guardian

AI-powered sleep and fatigue analysis based on your real shift pattern. Monitors average sleep, consecutive night runs, and sleep debt — then gives you personalised recommendations before burnout hits.

Fatigue score Sleep debt tracking Consecutive nights Wellness tips
✈️

Leave & Availability

Track Annual Leave, Comp Off, and Standby duties with a full balance overview for the year. Log swap requests, override rotations, and keep everything in one place.

Annual leave Comp off Standby duties Swap log Balance overview
🔄

Custom Rotation Patterns

Every rotation is different. Build a fully custom pattern with any combination of Day, Night, Noon, and Off segments in any sequence. Save multiple patterns and switch between them instantly.

Unlimited segments Live preview Save & name patterns Quick switch
